TecBooks is an interactive financial education platform designed for the R&EIT department at Tecnologico de Monterrey campus Querétaro. It gives professors the tools to organize classrooms and run financial analysis games, while students upload real business spreadsheets and explore income statements, balance sheets, cash flows, forecasts, and investment simulations — all in one place.

## What TecBooks does

TecBooks brings together three interconnected modules under one platform:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Financial dashboard" icon="table-columns">
    Upload an Excel workbook with your business data and get instant income statements, balance sheets, cash flow summaries, and forward-looking forecasts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simulators" icon="flask">
    Use the standalone Sales Forecasting and Project Evaluation simulators to run what-if analyses using statistical and financial models — no spreadsheet required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Classroom tools" icon="graduation-cap">
    Professors create classes, form student groups, and launch financial analysis games. Students join, submit work, and review results — all within a structured classroom context.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it's organized

TecBooks uses a multi-tenant model. Each institution has its own space accessed via a unique slug (`/mxrep/<your-institution>`). Within that space, admins manage professors and students, professors run classes and games, and students participate in learning activities.

<Note>
  TecBooks is designed for use within Tecnologico de Monterrey. Institution registration requires approval from a super-admin before professors and students can join.
</Note>
