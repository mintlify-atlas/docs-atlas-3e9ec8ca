---
title: "Register as a professor on TecBooks"
description: "Step-by-step guide for professors to register on TecBooks: select your institution, submit a request, and get approved by the institution admin."
---

Professors on TecBooks create and manage classes, configure production-line simulator games, and track student progress through the financial dashboard. Before you can do any of that, your institution must already be registered on TecBooks and you must request an account through the registry. Your institution's admin will review and approve your request.

<Note>
  An approved institution must exist before you can register as a professor. If your institution is not yet on TecBooks, ask whoever manages your institution to follow the [institution registration guide](/getting-started/register-institution) first.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Obtain your institution's slug">
    Ask your institution's TecBooks admin for the institution **slug** — the short identifier chosen during institution registration (e.g., `tec-monterrey`). You will need this to find your institution in the registration form. The admin can find it in their admin panel or in the welcome email they received when the institution was approved.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to the registry">
    Go to `/mxrep/registry`. On the "Choose your user type" screen, click the **Professors** card to open the Professor Registry form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the registration form">
    Complete all required fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Institution** | Select your institution from the dropdown. The list shows all approved institutions. If your institution does not appear, it has not been approved yet. |
    | **School Email** | Your institutional email address (must match your institution's email domain). |
    | **First Name(s)** | Your given name(s). |
    | **Last Name(s)** | Your family name(s). |
    | **Department** | The academic department you belong to (e.g., `Finance`, `Accounting`). |

    Click **Submit** to send your registration request.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email">
    After a successful submission, TecBooks sends a verification email to the address you provided. Check your inbox — including your spam folder — and follow the instructions in the email to confirm your address and proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Admin reviews and approves your request">
    Your institution's admin receives your request in their inbox at `/mxrep/<slug>/admin-panel/inbox`. They will review your details and either approve or decline the request. You will be notified by email with the outcome.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your password and log in">
    Once approved, you will receive an email with a link to finalize your account. Follow that link to choose a password, then log in at `/mxrep/auth/login` using your school email and new password. You will be taken directly to your professor panel at `/mxrep/<slug>/professor-panel`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The approval step is performed by your institution's admin, not by TecBooks support. If your request is taking longer than expected, reach out to your institution's TecBooks admin directly.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Once you are logged in, your first action should be to create a class and add students to it. From your professor panel you can create classes, configure simulator games, organize student groups, and monitor performance through the financial dashboard. See the guide below to get started.
</Tip>
