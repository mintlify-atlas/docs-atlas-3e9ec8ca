---
title: "Register as a student on TecBooks"
description: "Step-by-step guide for students to create a TecBooks account, join their institution with a slug, and start participating in financial analysis games."
---

TecBooks lets students participate in production-line simulator games and explore financial dashboards built around real accounting concepts. To get started, your professor or institution admin needs to give you two pieces of information: your institution's **slug** and confirmation that your institutional email address is registered. With those in hand, the registration process takes just a few minutes.

<Note>
  Your institution must already be approved on TecBooks before you can register. Your professor will let you know when the platform is ready for students.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your institution's slug from your professor">
    Ask your professor or institution admin for the institution **slug** — the short identifier for your school on TecBooks (e.g., `tec-monterrey`). You will need this to select your institution during registration. You will also need access to the institutional email address your professor has on file for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to the registry">
    Go to `/mxrep/registry`. On the "Choose your user type" screen, click the **Students** card to open the Student Registry form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the registration form">
    Complete the two required fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Institution** | Select your institution from the dropdown using the slug your professor gave you. |
    | **School Email** | Your institutional email address as registered by your professor or admin. |

    Click **Submit** to send your registration request.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email and verify your address">
    TecBooks sends a verification email to the address you provided. Open it and click the link inside to confirm your email and continue setting up your account. If the email does not arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finalize your account">
    The verification link takes you to the account finalization page. Enter your:

    - **First Name(s)**
    - **Last Name(s)**
    - **New Password** and **Confirm Password**

    Your institution and email are pre-filled and read-only. Click **Finish** to create your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in and join a game">
    Go to `/mxrep/auth/login` and sign in with your school email and the password you just set. You will land in your student panel at `/mxrep/<slug>/student-panel`, where you can view available games and join those your professor has assigned to you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After registering, explore the student panel to view your active games, check your performance metrics in the financial dashboard, and review past results. Ask your professor to add you to a class and assign you to a game group so you can start playing.
</Tip>
