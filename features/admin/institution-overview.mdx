Institution admins are the bridge between TecBooks and your Tecnologico de Monterrey campus. Each institution operates under its own URL space at `/mxrep/<slug>`, and your admin account is the authority within that boundary. You control who can teach, monitor registration requests, and keep your institution's profile and user base in order.

<Note>
  TecBooks has two distinct admin tiers. The **institution admin** (covered here) manages a single institution's professors and students. The **super admin** operates at the platform level and manages institution registration requests across all schools — institution admins do not have access to the super admin panel at `/mxrep/super-admin-panel`.
</Note>

## What you can do as an admin

Your admin panel at `/mxrep/<slug>/admin-panel` gives you access to the following areas:

- **Professors** — view all professors affiliated with your institution, invite new ones, and open individual professor profiles to edit their details or grant admin privileges.
- **Students** — browse your full student roster in a paginated table, and open individual student records to review their account status or remove them from the institution.
- **Inbox** — receive and act on professor registration requests. When a professor completes their registration after receiving your invitation, a notification appears here for your review.
- **Profile** — manage your own admin account details and institution information.
